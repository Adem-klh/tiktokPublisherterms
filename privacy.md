# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how this application ("App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our content automation service.

## 2. Information We Collect

This App may collect and process the following data:

- **TikTok account identifiers** (open ID, access tokens) required to publish content on your behalf via the TikTok API.
- **Video metadata** (titles, descriptions, hashtags) that you configure for publishing.
- **Usage logs** stored locally on your own device for debugging purposes.

We do **not** collect passwords, payment information, or any sensitive personal data.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

- Authenticate your TikTok account via OAuth 2.0.
- Publish video content to TikTok on your behalf.
- Maintain local logs for troubleshooting.

## 4. Data Storage

All data (tokens, logs, video files) is stored **locally on your own machine**. We do not operate remote servers or databases that store your personal information.

## 5. Third-Party Services

This App interacts with the **TikTok API**. Your use of TikTok's services is subject to TikTok's own [Privacy Policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/global/privacy-policy/en).

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

## 7. Data Retention

Access tokens are stored locally and can be deleted at any time by removing the corresponding token files from your device.

## 8. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, as tokens are stored locally, you are responsible for securing access to your device.

## 9. Your Rights

You may at any time:

- Revoke the App's access to your TikTok account via TikTok's privacy settings.
- Delete all locally stored data by removing the App's files from your device.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available in our GitHub repository.

## 11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
